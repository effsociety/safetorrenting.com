How do I use a cloud torrent service to download torrents safely?

Cloud torrent services are one way to download torrents safely and anonymously. Instead of downloading a torrent yourself, a cloud torrent service can download it for you, to a secure cloud storage. Then, you can safely download the content from their servers via HTTPS or even streaming. The introduction of the cloud service as a middleman means that your IP address is never associated with the torrent download, so the download cannot be traced back to you.

An extra benefit of cloud torrent services is the very fast download speeds. A typical torrent can be downloaded to the cloud within a matter of seconds.

Step 2: Download to the cloud

Find the torrent you wish to download as you usually do, for example by using the Sky Torrents search engine. Copy the magnet link address.

Then log into your cloud service and paste the link as a new download. If a magnet URL is not available for the torrent, then download the .torrent file and upload it into your cloud service for download.

Step 3: Download or stream to your computer

Once the download completes, which is typically in a matter of seconds, you can then safely download the files from the cloud. In the case of audio or video files, you can even stream the content directly to your computer, mobile device, or TV.
