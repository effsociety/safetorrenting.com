How do I use a VPN to download torrents safely?

VPN stands for Virtual Private Network. Using a VPN will hide your IP address in all your online activity. It will also have the further advantage of bypassing site blocking and bans on P2P traffic that your ISP may have in place.

When you connect to the internet through a VPN, your true IP address is hidden from the remote server. Further, all your traffic is encrypted and therefore hidden from your ISP. Therefore, not only will a VPN safeguard your privacy and anonymity while downloading torrents, in fact it will keep all your browsing activity private.

Step 2: Download the VPN client and connect to the VPN

After signing up with the VPN service you chose, you should download their client to your computer or mobile device. Follow their instructions to setup the client and connect to the VPN.

Step 3: Double-check that you are secured

To make sure that your VPN has been set up correct, click here and note the IP address. This should be different than your real IP address.

If you are not sure what your real IP address was, disconnect from the VPN and click here to check it. Then re-connect to the VPN and make sure that it’s showing a different IP address. If this is the case, then you are secured. If not, contact your VPN service for help.

Step 4: Download your torrent

Now that your browsing has been secured, go ahead and download your torrent as your normally would. You can rest assured that the download cannot be traced to your real IP address.
